Products

Protect

Our proprietary Croquis tool detects creative works inside AI training datasets and models. We search the giant public datasets used to train AI, and we probe the models themselves—across modalities, domains, and scales. If work has been taken, we can show it, with evidence.

Protection isn’t one-size-fits-all. The same technology works for a single artist watching over their own portfolio, a rights organization managing thousands of works, and a legal team that needs findings solid enough to build a case on.

Features

Find the work

We look for names and works in public training datasets and probe AI models directly using membership inference—the methods we explain in plain language in our FAQ.

Evidence you can act on

Transparent, per-work reports with supporting evidence: which datasets and models we checked, what we found, and how confident we are.

Keep watch

As new models and datasets appear, the analysis runs again. And for work published on your own sites, we add safeguards at the source to resist scraping.
