For AI Companies

We believe in a clearer and more collaborative future—one that gives agency to creatives and AI innovators alike. For AI companies, that means a straightforward way to license creative work whose rights holders have chosen to make it available for training and generation.

Our team works on technological solutions and standards for artists’ attribution, compensation, and representation—including published research on data provenance for generative AI datasets. We connect you with creators and rights holders who are ready to partner.

What you get

License data, cleanly

Explore creative work—across modalities and domains—that artists and rights holders have explicitly chosen to license for AI training and down-stream generation.

Provenance you can stand behind

Our research includes a data provenance framework for generative AI datasets, so consent and sourcing are documented rather than assumed.

Partner with creators

Compensation flows to the people who make the work that makes AI work—turning rights holders from adversaries into partners.
