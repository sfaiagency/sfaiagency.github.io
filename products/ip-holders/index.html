Products

For IP Holders

Studios, publishers, estates, and rights organizations manage catalogs that span thousands of works and many creators. Our technology can identify when large AI models have incorporated copyrighted work from your portfolio during their training or down-stream generation—at the scale you operate.

We provide transparent reports with supporting evidence for the works in your catalog, enabling you to address past usage and move towards a clearer and more collaborative future for the rights you manage.

What you get

Audit at portfolio scale

The same dataset search and membership inference we run for individual artists, applied across a managed portfolio of many assets—spanning modalities, domains, and scales.

Evidence for every work

Transparent reporting with supporting evidence across your catalog: which datasets and models were checked and what we found, so your team can act with confidence.

Managed licensing, on your terms

Explore licensing works from your catalog for AI training and generation—only if you so choose, with full control over which works, which partners, and on what terms.
