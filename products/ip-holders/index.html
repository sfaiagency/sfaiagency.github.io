Products

Enterprise

Studios, publishers, estates, and rights organizations manage catalogs that span thousands of works and creators. Our technology can identify when large AI models have incorporated copyrighted work from your portfolios during their training or down-stream generation—at the scale you operate.

Two simple steps: Protect: know where your work has gone and guard it going forward. License: decide if your work is available to be used for AI training and generation. And if so, under what conditions. Charge for it. Or, opt out completely.

Features

Protect

Know where the works in your catalog have gone—at the scale you operate.

  • Audit your full catalog: we search the giant public datasets used to train AI models and probe the models themselves, across modalities, domains, and scales.
  • Get transparent, per-work reports with supporting evidence, so your team can act with confidence.
  • Monitor continuously. As new models and datasets appear, the analysis runs again across your portfolio.
  • Add protection at the source, with extra safeguards for the works you publish across your own sites and platforms.

License

Decide whether—and under what conditions—any part of your catalog is available to AI.

  • Choose whether works from your catalog can be used for AI training or generation at all. Keeping everything off the table is always an option.
  • If you opt in, you set the conditions per work and per partner: scope, terms, and compensation.
  • Manage it as a portfolio: licensing decisions, terms, and revenue handled at the scale of many assets, not one work at a time.
  • Retain full control for the rights holders you represent—nothing is licensed without your decision.
