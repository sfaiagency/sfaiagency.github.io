Products

License

Whether—and how—creative work is available to AI should be the rights holder’s decision. Our licensing hub is the front door: rights holders choose what’s on the table, set the conditions, and get paid. AI companies license consented, documented data they can stand behind.

Nothing is licensed without a decision by the people who own the work—and saying no, to everything, is always an option.

Features

The rights holder’s terms

Per-work, per-partner conditions: which works, which uses—training, fine-tuning, or generation—on what terms, and for what compensation.

Consent and provenance documented

Our research includes a data provenance framework for generative AI datasets, so consent and sourcing are recorded from the start—ready for the disclosure rules now in force.

Both sides of the market

We connect rights holders who choose to license with AI companies that need clean data—so compensation flows to the people who make the work that makes AI work.

How it works

1

Rights holders set the terms

Choose what’s available for AI training and generation—work by work—and under what conditions. Or keep everything off the table.

2

3

License, with compensation built in

Terms are agreed directly, and attribution and compensation flow back to the rights holders—every time.
