Individual creators

Our unique technology can identify when large AI models have used your work during their training or down-stream generation. You get a report with the evidence—and the agency to decide what happens next. See a sample report here.

Two simple steps: Protect: know where your work has gone and guard it going forward. License: decide if your work is available to be used for AI training and generation. And if so, under what conditions. Charge for it. Or, opt out completely.

Features

Protect

Know where your work has gone—and keep it from going further without you.

  • See where your work appeared: we look for your name and your work in the giant public datasets used to train AI models, and we probe the models themselves using membership inference—the methods we explain in plain language in our FAQ.
  • Get a transparent report with supporting evidence: which datasets and models we checked, what we found, and how confident we are.
  • Stay protected over time. As new models and datasets appear, the analysis can run again—so your protection keeps up.
  • Add protection at the source, with extra safeguards for the work you publish on your own website to resist scraping for AI training.

License

Decide whether—and under what conditions—your work is available to AI. The choice is entirely yours.

  • Choose whether your work can be used for AI training or generation at all. Saying no, to everything, is always an option.
  • If you opt in, you set the conditions: which works, which partners, and on what terms.
  • Get compensated. We’re building a clearinghouse so creators who choose to license their work for AI training and generation get paid.
  • Stay in full control. You can change your mind, and nothing happens without your say-so.
