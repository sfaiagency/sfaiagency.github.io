For Artists

Our unique technology can identify when large AI models have incorporated your copyrighted work during their training or down-stream generation. You get a transparent report with supporting evidence—and the agency to decide what happens next.

Whether AI feels like a threat or an opportunity, the first step is the same: knowing where your work has gone. From there, the choice is yours—address past usage, get protected going forward, or license your work for AI training and generation, only if you so choose, on your terms.

What you get

Know where your work went

We look for your name and your work in the giant public datasets used to train AI models, and we probe the models themselves using membership inference—the same methods we explain in plain language in our FAQ.

A report you can act on

You receive a transparent report with supporting evidence: which datasets and models we checked, what we found, and how confident we are—enabling you to address past usage.

License only if you choose

We are building a clearinghouse so creators who want to license their work for AI training and generation can get paid—with full control over which works, which partners, and on what terms.
