About SFAI.agency

SFAI.agency is the Artificial Intelligence Agency of San Francisco. We build technology that identifies when large AI models have incorporated copyrighted work during their training. Our transparent reports provide artists with supporting evidence, enabling them to address past usage and move towards a clearer, more collaborative future â€” one that gives agency to creatives and AI innovators alike.

We believe that the rise of AI should empower artists, not diminish them. By bringing transparency to how creative works are used in AI training, we help protect the rights and livelihoods of the people who make the work that makes AI work.
