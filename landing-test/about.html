The San Francisco Artificial Intelligence Agency

Our Mission: "Agency"

We believe artists should have agency over how their work is used in the age of AI. Our mission is to provide the tools, technology, and advocacy that empower creators to protect their visual artistic style.

Founded in San Francisco in 2024, we've assembled a team of technologists, artists, and legal experts dedicated to ensuring that AI development respects creative rights.

Our Approach

We combine cutting-edge machine learning with a deep respect for artistic integrity. Our patented detection technology can identify stylistic fingerprints across millions of AI-generated images.

But technology alone isn't enough. We work closely with artists, studios, and legal teams to ensure our tools translate into real protection and fair compensation.
