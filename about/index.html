About SFAI.agency

At the Artificial Intelligence Agency of San Francisco (SFAI.agency for short), we build technology for protecting and advancing artists' agency in the landscape of AI. Our team brings together artists, technologists, AI researchers, and journalists, working on technological solutions as well as standards for artists' attribution, compensation, and representation.

We believe that the rise of AI should empower artists, not diminish them. By bringing transparency to how creative works are used in AI training and down-stream generation, we help protect the rights and livelihoods of the people who make the work that makes AI work.
